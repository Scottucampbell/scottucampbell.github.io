About me

Engineer, problem-solver, and lifelong tinkerer.

I'm a Senior Mechanical Design Engineer with 8+ years of experience, including 5+ years focused on electro-mechanical medical devices. I'm a named inventor on the patented Dolphin™ Electric Ring Cutter — a device that has now helped treat over 45,000 patients across North America.

I've worked across the full product development lifecycle: from early user research and concept design all the way through to production, V&V, and regulatory compliance (ISO 14971, IEC 60601).

I genuinely love the challenge of making things intuitive. I keep coming back to something Dieter Rams said — "Good design is as little design as possible." That's the lens I bring to every design problem.
